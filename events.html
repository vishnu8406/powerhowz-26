INNOVISTA

Paper Presentation

General Rules
  • Open to UG and PG students.
  • Topics must be related to engineering or technology.
  • Team size: Minimum 1 and maximum 4 members.
  • Papers must be original and properly cited.
  • full papers must be submitted before the deadline.
  • Presentations should contain a maximum of 10–12 slides.
Presentation Rules
  • Presentation duration: 5 minutes followed by 3 minutes of Q&A.
  • Participants must explain the content and not read directly from slides.
  • Minimal use of videos and animations is recommended.
  • Presentation files must be uploaded through the Google Form in PPT or PDF format.
  • Participants are advised to keep backup files.
  • Participants may bring a hard copy of their paper.
  • Participants must report at least 15 minutes before their allotted slot.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.

POWERPLAY

Technical Quiz Event

POWERPLAY is an interactive quiz event with fun and engaging rounds designed to challenge participants through speed, strategy, and quick thinking.

Team Size: 2 – 3 Members

Rules & Regulations
ROUND 1: POWER KAHOOT (20 Questions)

(Online Preliminary Round)

  • Consists of multiple-choice questions.
  • Participants must join using the provided Kahoot PIN.
  • Each question is time-bound.
  • Top-scoring teams will be shortlisted for the next round.
ROUND 2: STRIKE & ANSWER (15 Questions)

(Rapid Fire + Skill Task Round)

  • Questions will be displayed on the screen.
  • If a team knows the answer, one member must throw the ball into the cup.
  • Only after a successful throw, the team is allowed to answer.
  • The fastest team to complete the task gets the first chance.
  • No passing of questions to other teams.
ROUND 3: SPIN THE TOPIC

(Final Written Round)

  • Each team will spin the wheel once to select a topic.
  • Topics include Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Power Electronics, Measurements, Renewable Energy, etc.
  • A question paper consisting of 5 questions will be provided based only on the selected topic.
  • Time allotted: 10 minutes.
  • No change of topic or question paper is allowed.
  • Answers will be evaluated by the judges.
  • Judges’ decision is final.

BROCODE

Technical Quiz Event

Decode brands.
Outthink the visuals.

BroCode is an engaging and fast-paced technical quiz event designed to test participants’ brand awareness, visual intelligence, and logical thinking. The event challenges participants to think quickly, observe keenly, and decode visual clues under time pressure. With a blend of rapid-fire brand recognition and image-based reasoning, BroCode promises excitement, competitiveness, and a true test of smart thinking.

Event Format
Round 1 – Rapid Fire (Logo Hunt)
  • Participants will be shown a series of brand logos.
  • Each logo will appear for a short duration.
  • Participants must identify and answer the correct brand name.
  • Speed and accuracy are crucial.
  • Top-scoring participants will qualify for the next round.
Round 2 – Visual IQ (Image-Based Quiz)
  • A single image will be displayed on the screen.
  • One or more questions related to the image will be asked.
  • Questions may test observation skills, logical reasoning, or general awareness.
  • Participants must analyze the image carefully and answer within the given time.
  • Winners will be selected based on correctness and response time.
